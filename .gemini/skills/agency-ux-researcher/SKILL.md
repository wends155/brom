# UX Researcher

## When to Use
Load this skill during the Intent & Discovery phase of the `/design` workflow (Step 1) when GUI or Assets mode is active, to structure clarifying questions around target demographics and user journeys.

## User Persona Template
*   **Demographics**: Age Range, Location, Occupation, Tech Proficiency, Device Preferences
*   **Behavioral Patterns**: Usage Frequency, Task Priorities, Decision Factors, Pain Points, Motivations
*   **Goals & Needs**: Primary Goals, Secondary Goals, Success Criteria, Information Needs
*   **Context of Use**: Environment, Time Constraints, Distractions, Social Context

## User Journey Map Framework
*   **Touchpoints**: Key interaction points throughout the experience
*   **Pain Points**: Friction areas and barriers to task completion
*   **Emotions**: User feelings and sentiment at each touchpoint
*   **Opportunities**: Strategic areas for feature or workflow improvement

## Usability Review Checklist
*(Composed from UX research principles)*
1.  **Task Completion**: Can users complete primary tasks without assistance?
2.  **Error Prevention & Recovery**: Are errors minimized, clear, and is recovery straightforward?
3.  **Visibility of System Status**: Does the system keep users informed of its current state?
4.  **Match Real World**: Does the design speak the users' language and follow real-world conventions?
5.  **User Control & Freedom**: Are there clear "cancel," "undo," and "exit" options?
6.  **Consistency & Standards**: Are patterns and terminology consistent throughout the interface?
7.  **Recognition over Recall**: Are objects, actions, and options visible to minimize cognitive load?
8.  **Flexibility & Efficiency**: Are there accelerators and shortcuts for power users?
9.  **Aesthetic & Minimalist Design**: Is irrelevant or rarely needed UI noise eliminated?
10. **Help & Documentation**: Is guidance contextually relevant and easily accessible when needed?

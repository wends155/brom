# Brand Guardian

## When to Use
Load this skill during the Intent & Discovery phase of the `/design` workflow (Step 1) when GUI or Assets mode is active, to establish brand consistency constraints.

## Brand Foundation Framework
- **Brand Purpose**: Why the brand exists beyond making profit - the meaningful impact
- **Brand Vision**: Aspirational future state - where the brand is heading
- **Brand Mission**: What the brand does and for whom - the specific value delivery
- **Brand Values**: Core principles that guide all brand behavior
- **Brand Promise**: Commitment to customers and stakeholders

## Brand Personality Parameters
*(Composed from brand identity principles)*
- **Formal ↔ Playful**: Determine the level of professional rigidity vs. casual approachability.
- **Traditional ↔ Modern**: Establish the stylistic era anchoring the brand.
- **Reserved ↔ Bold**: Dictate the volume and intensity of visual expressions.
- **Accessible ↔ Exclusive**: Define the barriers and inclusiveness of the brand vibe.

## Visual Identity Constraints
*(Composed from design system principles)*
- **Color Mood**: e.g., Warm/Cool/Neutral - dictates the emotional resonance of the palette.
- **Typography Mood**: e.g., Modern/Classic/Playful - drives font family selection.
- **Imagery Style**: e.g., Photography/Illustration/Abstract - guides visual asset choice.
- **Shape Language**: e.g., Soft/Sharp/Organic - influences border radius and geometry.
